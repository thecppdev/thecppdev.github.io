The Full Monty License

I was looking for a license to distribute the code. I wanted a license that allow you, dear developer, to do whatever you want to do with the code examples. The kind of all-permissive license. However, each one of the licenses that I analysed requires the programmer to keep the license text intact, or keep the names of the copyright holders, or something else.

But I wanted for you the license I wish I had found whenever I been looking for an example code. Therefore I made it and name it the Full Monty License. This is all its text,


            This program is free software. You may use and modify this program, 
            free of charge, for any purpose you want.

            You may also, distribute the totality, a part or a derived work
            of this program under the Full Monty License or any other terms,
            at your choice.

            This program is distributed in the hope that it will be useful,
            but without any warranty; without even the implied warranty of
            merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose.

I wish it makes your development free!
